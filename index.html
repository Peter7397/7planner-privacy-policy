7Planner Privacy Policy

Effective Date: October 11, 2025
Last Updated: October 11, 2025

Overview

7Planner ("we," "our," or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal information when you use the 7Planner application.

Information Collection

Types of Information We Collect

1. Account Information

  • Email address (for account creation and login)
  • Username (optional)
  • Password (encrypted storage)

2. App Usage Data

  • Life roles information you create
  • Tasks and to-do items
  • Schedules and reminders
  • App usage preferences
  • In-app activity logs

3. Device Information

  • Device type and operating system version
  • App version information
  • Crash reports and performance data

4. Technical Data

  • IP address (for basic connectivity)
  • App usage statistics
  • Error logs

Information We Do NOT Collect

  • Location information
  • Contact information
  • Photos or media files
  • Call records
  • SMS content
  • Usage of other applications

Information Use

How We Use Your Information

1. Core Services

  • Create and manage your personal account
  • Store and manage your life roles and tasks
  • Provide task reminders and notifications
  • Sync data to your devices

2. App Improvement

  • Analyze app usage to improve features
  • Fix bugs and performance issues
  • Develop new features

3. Customer Support

  • Respond to your inquiries and requests
  • Provide technical support
  • Resolve account-related issues

Data Storage and Security

Data Storage Locations

  • Your personal data is primarily stored on your device
  • Account information is encrypted and stored on secure servers
  • Data backups are stored in encrypted cloud storage

Security Measures

  • Use industry-standard encryption for data transmission
  • Implement access controls and authentication
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Limit employee access to personal data

Data Retention

  • Account data: Retained until you delete your account
  • Usage logs: Retained for up to 12 months
  • Crash reports: Retained for up to 6 months

Data Sharing

We do NOT share your personal information, except:

1. Legal Requirements

  • When required by government or law enforcement
  • To comply with court orders or legal processes
  • To protect our rights and property

2. Service Providers

  • Only with necessary third-party service providers
  • These providers must adhere to the same privacy standards
  • We do NOT sell your personal information

We will NOT:

  • Sell your personal information to third parties
  • Share your personal data with advertisers
  • Use your data for marketing purposes (unless you explicitly consent)

Your Rights

You have the right to:

1. Access Your Data

  • View information we have collected about you
  • Request copies of your data

2. Correct Data

  • Update or correct inaccurate information
  • Modify your account settings

3. Delete Data

  • Delete your account and related data
  • Request deletion of specific information

4. Data Portability

  • Export your data in common formats
  • Transfer data to other services

5. Restrict Processing

  • Limit how we process your data
  • Opt out of certain data uses

Children's Privacy

7Planner is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we discover we have collected such information, we will delete it immediately.

International Data Transfers

Your data may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your own. We ensure all international transfers comply with applicable data protection laws.

Third-Party Services

Third-party services we use:

1. Cloud Storage Services

  • For data backup and synchronization
  • Bound by strict data protection agreements

2. Analytics Services

  • To understand app usage patterns
  • Does not include personally identifiable information

3. Crash Reporting Services

  • To identify and fix app issues
  • Contains only technical information, no personal data

Privacy Policy Updates

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Significant changes will be communicated through:

  • In-app notifications
  • Email notifications
  • App store update descriptions

Continued use of the app constitutes acceptance of the updated privacy policy.

Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this privacy policy, please contact us:

Email: privacy@7planner.app
In-app Support: Through "Settings" > "Help & Support" in the app

We will respond to your inquiry within 30 days.

Legal Basis

This privacy policy is based on the following legal frameworks:

  • Personal Information Protection Law of the People's Republic of China
  • Cybersecurity Law of the People's Republic of China
  • European Union General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR)
  • California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA)
